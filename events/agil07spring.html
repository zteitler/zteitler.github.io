AGIL: Algebraic Geometry In Louisiana

Saturday, April 14, 2007

Hosted by Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana

This is the second of a continuing series of regional conferences in algebraic geometry. Algebraic geometers and researchers in related areas are invited from any location, and especially from Louisiana.

Invited speakers:

  • Tai Huy Ha, Tulane
  • Michael Joyce, Tulane
  • Helena Verrill, LSU

For any questions, please contact the organizers: A. Vitter at vitter@math.tulane.edu or Z. Teitler at zteitler@selu.edu.

Registration:

There will be no registration fee, or "formal" registration. If you are interested in attending, please email Z. Teitler at zteitler@selu.edu so we can estimate attendance and send further information. We are unable to provide travel support.
